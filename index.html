Welcome to PetMatch!

Your perfect pet companion is just a few clicks away.
Whether you're looking to adopt or help a pet find a loving home, PetMatch connects kind-hearted people with animals in need — safely, locally, and easily.

How It Works:

Create Your Profile

Sign up and tell us what kind of pets you love — dogs, cats, bunnies, or something more exotic. You can even upload a photo so shelters and fellow adopters know who you are.

Browse Available Pets

Explore our growing list of adoptable animals, complete with photos, personality notes, and location. Filter by age, breed, or size to find your ideal match.

List A Pet for Adoption

Need to rehome a pet? Our simple upload form lets you post a pet's details and photo. Local adopters can connect with you directly through your listing.

Make The Connection

Once you’ve found your match, reach out through secure messaging to schedule a meet-up, ask questions, or finalize the adoption.
